What is Fantasy Football?

Fantasy football is a virtual game where participants create their own football teams using real NFL players. Players earn points based on the real-life performance of their selected players in NFL games. The goal is to score more points than your opponents over a season or weekly matchups. Participants can manage their teams by trading players, adding free agents, or adjusting their lineups based on matchups and injuries.
