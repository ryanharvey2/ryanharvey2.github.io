My League

I'm part of a 12 man league. This is our first year doing a 12 man, we typically have been a 10 man league. I'm not sure why exactly we swtiched to a 12 man league, or even where the new players came from, but it has been fun so far. I spoke to our leagues commissioner, Tanner, about the status of the league and he said he would like to go back to a 10 man league next season. I have found the 12 man league to be slightly more difficult due to the fact that there are two more teams with 15 man rosters plus an IR spot. It just makes it more difficult to put together a deep team, but it also forces players to be more active if they are in it to win it.
